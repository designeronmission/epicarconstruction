Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our residential construction services.

How long does a typical residential construction project take?

The timeline for residential construction projects varies based on size, complexity, and specific requirements. A standard single-family home typically takes 6-12 months from planning to completion. Custom luxury homes or larger projects may require 12-18 months. We provide a detailed project timeline during the planning phase and maintain regular updates throughout construction.

Do you provide design services or work with architects?

Yes, we offer comprehensive design-build services with our in-house architectural team. We can also collaborate with your chosen architect to ensure seamless integration between design and construction. Our design services include conceptual planning, detailed architectural drawings, 3D visualizations, and interior design coordination.

What is included in your residential construction pricing?

Our pricing is transparent and comprehensive, covering all aspects of the construction process. This includes site preparation, foundation work, structural construction, roofing, exterior finishes, plumbing, electrical, HVAC systems, interior finishes, and basic landscaping. We provide detailed breakdowns and work with you to select materials and finishes that align with your budget while maintaining quality standards.

Do you offer warranties on your residential construction work?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties on all our residential construction projects. This includes a 10-year structural warranty, 2-year workmanship warranty, and manufacturer warranties on all installed materials and systems. We believe in standing behind our work and ensuring long-term satisfaction for our clients.

Can you work with my existing architectural plans?

Absolutely. We regularly work with clients who have existing architectural plans. Our team will review the plans, provide value engineering suggestions if needed, and ensure they comply with local building codes. We can also make modifications or enhancements based on your preferences and our construction expertise.
